## Rollups ([[Optimistic Rollups]], [[ZK-Rollups]])

Remember our expressway analogy? Rollups are like expressways that can handle a high volume of traffic. They take a large number of transactions off the main network, process them, and then roll them up into a single transaction that's recorded back on the [[Layer 1 - The Base Layer|Layer 1]] [[Blockchain Technology|blockchain]].

There are two main types of rollups: [[Optimistic Rollups]] and [[ZK-Rollups]]. They both work similarly but use different methods to verify the validity of transactions.
